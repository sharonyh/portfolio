Fodmappa

During the fall semester of my sophomore year, I was enrolled in a graphic design course through the School of Information. For one of my projects, I had to envision a branding guideline and a high-fidelity digital prototype for an app. The company I was designing for was a health app which tracked your daily dietary intake and made suggestions based on your eating habits. It also served as a digital grocery list.
